Challenges Marketplace is a financial data management platform that serves both SMEs and their potential investors. Challenges Marketplace enables SMEs to gather, manage, and analyze their own data, including the monthly and annual financial data that investors expect to see. Additionally, the platform provides a visualization dashboard for the CEO and management team to see all their key metrics in one place. Harvard’s management guru Peter Drucker says:“you can’t manage what you can’t measure.” This dashboard will help SME managers—who often have limited financial and data analysis processes— better understand and manage their businesses. Moreover, the platform helps those SMEs who are ready for equity or debt investment to show their best side to potential investors that they select.

In addition to serving the SMEs, Challenges Marketplace serves potential investors by providing them with a clear and standardized view of each company’s finances and other metrics. The platform enables investors to easily see SME data so that they can screen companies and choose which to focus on for further due diligence. For the first time, investors have access to SME data in developing countries so that they can diversify their investment portfolio and invest in socially responsible small businesses.
